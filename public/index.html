What is Hevi Explorer?

                  
Your device becomes a private media server you can open in any browser.

                

              

              
Hevi Explorer is a self-hosted file manager and media vault that runs entirely on your own machine using Node.js and Express. It turns your device — phone, laptop, PC, or server — into a local web server that you access from any browser on the same network.

              
Instead of uploading your personal files to a third-party cloud service, everything stays exactly where it already is. You browse your folders, stream your videos, play your music, view your images, open your documents, and transfer files across devices — all through a clean, modern web interface that feels like a premium app.

              
There are no account requirements, no subscriptions, no bandwidth limits, and no data ever leaves your device unless you explicitly create an optional remote tunnel. The vault is yours, the files are yours, and the server is yours.

            


            
            

              

                02
                

                  
Origin Story

                  
Built from a real frustration — a 4 GB file, a time limit, and no reliable tool to move it.

                

              

              

                
"

                
One day I had a 4 GB important and private file on my phone that I needed to transfer to my PC — fast. The clock was ticking. I opened WhatsApp first: 2 GB file size limit. Blocked. I tried Telegram next: also a 2 GB limit, and even within that limit the transfer speed was painfully slow. On top of that, both platforms route your file through their servers, which felt wrong for something that was genuinely private.

                
I had no good option. No tool that was truly local, truly unlimited, and truly fast. So instead of compromising, I built one myself.

                
Hevi Explorer was born from that exact frustration. No artificial file-size cap. No uploading to someone else's server. No waiting for slow cloud infrastructure. Just your device, your local Wi-Fi network, your browser, and the fastest transfer path that physically exists — a direct connection between two devices on the same network.

                
What started as a personal tool to solve one problem became a full-featured private media vault used on Android, Linux, Windows, and macOS.

Every tool you need to explore, preview, protect, and move your files — all in one place.

AeroGrab — Gesture-Powered File TransferA first-of-its-kind way to send files between devices using just your hand. Open a file, make a fist (✊) for ~1 second to "grab" it, then on the receiving device show an open palm (✋) to "catch" it. Built on real on-device hand AI (MediaPipe Tasks Vision), end-to-end peer-to-peer over WebRTC — files never touch a server. Designed and built from scratch by Afsar Ali (TWH) — exclusive to Hevi Explorer.

Works hand-in-hand with AeroGrab and the standard transfer flow.

Once installed it opens in its own window, lives on your home screen or app drawer, works offline for the UI shell, and behaves like a native app — no Play Store, no App Store, no installer.

When no folders are pinned, Active Folders automatically shows root-level folders as a smart fallback. Each device keeps its own pinned list.

How It Works

                  
A simple but powerful local pipeline — with optional secure internet access when you need it.

                

              

              

                

                  
1

                  
Start the local serverRunning node server.js on your device starts a lightweight Express HTTP server. It automatically detects your environment (Termux, Linux, Windows, Replit) and sets the root directory accordingly. It also scans for a free port starting at 5000.

                

                

                  
2

                  
File index is built in the backgroundAs soon as the server starts, it begins indexing your root folder in the background. The index is saved to disk so future startups are instant. A file watcher monitors for changes in real time and keeps the index updated automatically.

                

                

                  
3

                  
Open the browser interfaceNavigate to the displayed local URL in any browser. The vanilla JavaScript frontend loads instantly and communicates with the server's REST API endpoints to display your files, folders, and media in a clean, app-like interface.

                

                

                  
4

                  
Files stream directly from your storageWhen you open a video, image, audio, or document, the server streams it directly from your storage to your browser. Videos support range requests for seeking. Large files are streamed in chunks and never need to be fully loaded into memory.

                

                

                  
5

                  
Connect other devices on the same networkAny device on the same Wi-Fi or hotspot accesses the vault by opening the local IP address shown in the console, or by scanning the QR code. This enables full-speed LAN transfers between any two devices with no app needed.

                

                

                  
6

                  
Use WAN tunnel only when you need remote accessIf you need to reach your files from a different network, the built-in Cloudflare Tunnel integration creates a temporary encrypted HTTPS public link in seconds. Stop the tunnel at any time to immediately cut off outside access. No router configuration needed.

                

              

            


            
            

              

                05
                

                  
Data Safety & Privacy

                  
Your files never leave your device. Here is exactly what we collect and what we do not.

                

              

              

                

                  
                

                

                  
100% Local — Your files never leave your device

                  
Every file you browse, stream, or transfer stays entirely on the device running the server. Nothing is ever uploaded to any external server by Hevi Explorer itself.

                

              


              

                
What we do NOT do

                

                  
🚫

                  

                    We do NOT collect your files
                    
Your photos, videos, documents, and any other files you browse or stream through Hevi Explorer are never uploaded, copied, or sent anywhere. They stay on your device at all times.

                  

                

                

                  
🔐

                  

                    We do NOT require an account
                    
There is no sign-up, no login service, no email address, and no user profile of any kind. You run the app, you use it — that is all.

                  

                

                

                  
☁️

                  

                    We do NOT sync to the cloud
                    
Hevi Explorer is fully local. There is no automatic cloud backup, no sync service, and no third-party storage integration. Your files never leave your device unless you explicitly choose to create a WAN tunnel.

                  

                

              


              

                
What we may collect

                

                  
📊

                  

                    Minimal anonymous analytics only
                    
We may collect very small anonymous data: total download count, total user count, and device/platform type (e.g., "Android" or "Windows") — purely to understand which devices have issues and improve compatibility. No file names, no file contents, and no personal information is ever collected.

                  

                

              


              

                
What you control

                

                  
🛡️

                  

                    You control who can access the vault
                    
By default the vault is only accessible on your local network. You can optionally add a password lock, or create a temporary WAN tunnel link that you can stop at any time. You are always in full control of who can access your files.

                  

                

                

                  
🔗

                  

                    WAN tunnel is opt-in and temporary
                    
Cloudflare Tunnel is only activated when you tap "Start Tunnel" in settings. It creates a temporary public HTTPS URL. Stop it at any time to immediately remove outside access. The tunnel does not persist between server restarts.

Cloud Storage Integration

                  
Connect Google Drive, Dropbox, OneDrive, and MEGA — all inside Hevi Explorer. Your accounts, your data, zero compromise.

                

              


              
              

                
🔵Google Drive

                
🟦Dropbox

                
🟦OneDrive

                
🔴MEGA

              


              
Hevi Explorer is the only local-first file manager that seamlessly integrates four major cloud storage providers — Google Drive, Dropbox, OneDrive, and MEGA — directly into the same interface you use for your local files. No switching apps, no separate tabs. Your cloud files appear in the same clean grid layout as your device files. You browse, stream, and manage everything from one place.


              
              

                

                  
                  Why Cloud Storage in Hevi Explorer is 100% Safe
                

                

                  

                    🔐
                    

                      Your credentials never leave your device
                      
OAuth tokens and API keys for Google Drive, Dropbox, OneDrive, and MEGA are stored locally in encrypted form on the device running the server — inside data/profiles/. They are encrypted using a randomly generated server secret key unique to your installation. No credential is ever sent to any Hevi server — because there is no Hevi server.

                    

                  

                  

                    🛡️
                    

                      Files flow directly through your own account — not through us
                      
When you browse or stream a cloud file, Hevi Explorer makes the API request directly to Google, Dropbox, Microsoft, or MEGA using your own OAuth token. Your data travels from the cloud provider's servers to your browser. No third party ever touches your files.

                    

                  

                  

                    🔑
                    

                      OAuth-based login — no password ever stored
                      
For Google Drive, Dropbox, and OneDrive, authentication uses the official OAuth 2.0 flow. You log in directly on the provider's own website. Hevi Explorer only receives a time-limited access token — your password is never seen or stored by the app.

                    

                  

                  

                    ⚡
                    

                      Token refresh is automatic and local
                      
Access tokens expire after a short time. Hevi Explorer automatically refreshes them in the background using your stored refresh token — entirely locally, without any intervention or external service from our side.

                    

                  

                  

                    🗑️
                    

                      You can disconnect any account at any time
                      
Open Settings → Cloud Storage, tap the trash icon next to any connected account, and it is instantly removed. All stored tokens for that account are deleted from your device immediately. No traces remain.

                    

                  

                

              


              
              

                

                  
How to Connect Cloud Storage

                  
Step-by-step — takes under 5 minutes for any provider.

                

              

              

                

                  
1

                  
Open SettingsTap the ⚙️ gear icon in the bottom navigation bar or sidebar to open the Settings panel.

                

                

                  
2

                  
Go to Cloud StorageInside Settings, scroll down and tap Cloud Storage. You will see tiles for Google Drive, Dropbox, OneDrive, and MEGA.

                

                

                  
3

                  
Tap "Connect" on your providerEach provider has a Connect button. For Google Drive, Dropbox, and OneDrive — tapping Connect opens the official OAuth login page on that provider's website. For MEGA — you enter your MEGA email and password directly (MEGA uses its own end-to-end encrypted auth system).

                

                

                  
4

                  
Authorise access on the provider's pageLog in to your account on the provider's own website (Google, Microsoft, Dropbox). Grant the requested permissions. You are redirected back to Hevi Explorer automatically. Your token is saved encrypted on your device.

                

                

                  
5

                  
Access your cloud filesYour connected cloud account appears immediately in the sidebar and on the Home screen. Tap it to browse your cloud storage in the same grid view as local files. Stream videos and images directly without downloading. Open documents in the built-in viewers.

                

                

                  
6

                  
Share with other devices (optional)Connected cloud accounts can be set to "Shared" mode. When shared, any device that opens your Hevi vault on the same network can browse and stream from that cloud account — no separate login needed on those devices.

Why Hevi's Cloud Integration is Different

                

                  

                    ✅
                    
4 providers in one place
Most file managers support at most one cloud service. Hevi supports Google Drive, Dropbox, OneDrive, and MEGA simultaneously — in the same interface.

                  

                  

                    ✅

Unlike apps that route cloud traffic through their own servers, Hevi makes API calls directly from your device to your cloud provider. Your data never passes through any Hevi infrastructure.

                    ✅

Most tools force you to download cloud files before viewing. Hevi streams videos and images directly from the cloud API to your browser player — faster and with zero local storage used.

                  

                  

                    ✅
                    
Multiple accounts per provider
Need access to two different Google Drive accounts or two MEGA accounts? Hevi supports connecting multiple accounts from the same provider at the same time — a feature most file managers completely lack.

                  

                  

                    ✅
                    
Seamless LAN sharing
Shared cloud accounts extend to every device on your local network or WAN tunnel. One person authenticates; everyone on the vault benefits. No repeated logins across devices.

PWA · Install Guide

            
How to install Hevi as an app

            
Pick your device — full step-by-step instructions below.

          

        


        

          
          
          
        


        


          
          

            
✅ Best browsers: Chrome, Edge, Brave, Samsung Internet. Firefox works too but uses a slightly different menu.

            
    
              
  1. 1
    Open Hevi Explorer in Chrome (or Edge / Brave)If you're already in the right browser, you're good. If not, copy the URL and open it there.
    2. 
              
  2. 2
    Tap the menu (⋮) at the top-rightThe three vertical dots inside your browser's address bar.
    3. 
              
  3. 3
    Tap "Install app" or "Add to Home screen"The exact label depends on your browser. Some browsers also show a small ⊕ icon in the address bar — tap that.
    4. 
              
  4. 4
    Confirm "Install"Hevi installs in seconds, lands on your home screen with the TWH icon, and opens in its own window — just like a normal app.
    5. 
            

            
Tip: After installing, open Hevi from the home screen — not the browser — to get the standalone app experience and offline UI.

          


          
          

            
⚠️ On iPhone / iPad, installing PWAs only works inside Safari. Chrome on iOS cannot install — Apple blocks it.

            
    
              
  1. 1
    Open Hevi Explorer in SafariNot Chrome, not Firefox — it must be Safari for the install option to appear.
    2. 
              
  2. 2
    Tap the Share buttonThe square with an arrow pointing up — bottom of the screen on iPhone, top-right on iPad.
    3. 
              
  3. 3
    Scroll down and tap "Add to Home Screen"Look for the icon that looks like a square with a + inside.
    4. 
              
  4. 4
    Tap "Add" in the top-rightHevi appears on your home screen with its own icon and opens fullscreen — no Safari address bar.
    5. 
            

          


          
          

            
✅ Works on Chrome, Edge, Brave, Opera, Arc on Windows, macOS, Linux, and ChromeOS.

            
    
              
  1. 1
    Look at the right side of the address barYou'll see a small install icon — usually a monitor with a downward arrow, or a ⊕ symbol.
    2. 
              
  2. 2
    Click that install iconIf you don't see it, click the menu (⋮ or …) in the top-right and choose "Install Hevi…" or "Apps → Install this site as an app".
    3. 
              
  3. 3
    Click "Install" in the popupHevi installs as a real desktop app — Start Menu / Launchpad / app drawer entry, taskbar pinning, and its own window.
    4. 
              
  4. 4
    Done — launch from your appsSearch "Hevi" in Start / Spotlight / your launcher and open it like any other app.
    5. 
            

            
Firefox desktop currently does not support installing PWAs natively. Use Chrome, Edge, Brave, or Arc for the install option.

Quick Tips

          
A few simple habits make AeroGrab feel instant — on phone or PC.

          

            

              
📷

              
Stay in frameKeep your hand close to the camera, palm or fist fully visible.

            

            

              
⏱️

              
Hold ~1 secondDon't flash the gesture — hold steady until the bar fills.

            

            

              
✋

              
Relax betweenLower or relax your hand briefly before the next gesture.

            

            

              
👀

              
Watch the labelThe live status under the camera tells you exactly what AeroGrab sees.
